Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Justin Salcedo, a software engineer and passionate programmer in San Diego, California. For more than five years, I have developed full-stack web and mobile applications. I enjoy creating technological solutions for real-world problems for businesses and end users.

Recently, I orchestrated a cross-platform app's server and client architecture supporting cloud synchronization and third-party integration based on PaaS, IaaS, and container virtualization. I will launch my first commercial Android application to Google Play in September of this year.

For the last seven years, I have constantly moved out with many friends, relatives, and kind strangers, sharing new tastes and cultures. As a change-driven person, I'm ready to adapt to any place, circumstance, industry, and technology. Learning is not a chore but a pleasure and honor.
