Libellus Gallery

Created by Justin Salcedo and his development team, Libellus is a personal management and organization app that assists with daily events and tasks. Its schedule planning features help users recover precious time to do what they love. Free and with no ads, Libellus fights procrastination and ADHD with timers and notifications.
