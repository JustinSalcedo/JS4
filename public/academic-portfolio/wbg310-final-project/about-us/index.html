About Us

Well, it's just me

Hi there! I'm Justin Salcedo, a software engineer and passionate programmer from San Diego, California. I have developed full-stack web and mobile applications for more than five years. I enjoy creating technological solutions for real-world problems for businesses and end users.

Recently, I orchestrated a cross-platform app's server and client architecture supporting cloud synchronization and third-party integration based on PaaS, IaaS, and container virtualization. I launched my first commercial Android application to Google Play in September of this year.

I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems at DeVry University and expect to graduate in June 2025. During the last three years, I've enjoyed obtaining the knowledge and practice that DeVry has given me, and this site is proof of our professional preparation as students.
